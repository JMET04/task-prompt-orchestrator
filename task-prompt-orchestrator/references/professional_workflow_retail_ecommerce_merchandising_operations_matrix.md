# Professional Workflow Retail Ecommerce Merchandising Operations Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must run retail, ecommerce, marketplace, store, catalog, merchandising, pricing, promotion, omnichannel, customer experience, inventory, fulfillment, loss prevention, or retail performance operations.

## Retail Ecommerce Scope

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| RETSCOPE01 | What retail or ecommerce workflow is involved? | Define store, marketplace, DTC site, wholesale portal, omnichannel, franchise, pop-up, subscription, or service-retail context. |
| RETSCOPE02 | Who is the target shopper or buyer? | Capture segment, occasion, channel preference, price sensitivity, loyalty status, geography, device, and purchase barrier. |
| RETSCOPE03 | What product or category is in scope? | Specify SKU, category, collection, bundle, service, digital product, regulated item, seasonal line, or private label. |
| RETSCOPE04 | What channel mix applies? | Map physical store, ecommerce site, marketplace, social commerce, wholesale, app, call center, distributor, or affiliate channel. |
| RETSCOPE05 | What business objective drives the workflow? | Define revenue, margin, sell-through, basket size, conversion, retention, traffic, stock health, launch, liquidation, or brand goal. |
| RETSCOPE06 | What operational constraints apply? | Capture stock, lead time, shelf space, content readiness, channel rules, staffing, promotion calendar, compliance, and budget. |
| RETSCOPE07 | What customer promise must be protected? | Define price, availability, delivery, pickup, return, service level, authenticity, warranty, and support expectations. |
| RETSCOPE08 | What systems or data sources are involved? | Identify POS, ecommerce platform, PIM, OMS, WMS, ERP, CRM, CDP, marketplace portal, analytics, and loyalty systems. |
| RETSCOPE09 | What risk or compliance boundary applies? | Check consumer protection, pricing rules, product claims, age restriction, privacy, payments, tax, marketplace policies, and accessibility. |
| RETSCOPE10 | What final retail artifact is required? | Produce retail brief, launch plan, assortment plan, PDP checklist, promotion plan, store playbook, dashboard, or issue resolution packet. |
