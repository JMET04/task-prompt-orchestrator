# Professional Workflow Personal Tax Filing Records Notices Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal tax filing preparation, income documents, deduction and credit record organization, tax forms, filing calendars, tax professional handoff, e-filing records, refunds, payments, tax notices, audit or inquiry packets, multi-state/moving/nonresident boundaries, tax record retention, or annual tax review.

Use this for personal organization, source-backed checklists, document gathering, deadline tracking, communication drafts, recordkeeping, and professional handoff preparation. Do not use it to provide tax, legal, accounting, investment, payroll, immigration, benefits eligibility, or audit-defense advice, and do not replace a CPA, enrolled agent, tax preparer, attorney, payroll provider, government tax authority, or official tax software.

| TAXSCP03 | What jurisdictions may apply? | Note federal/national, state/province, city/local, school district, foreign, or nonresident filing possibilities for professional review. |

| TAXSCP06 | What filing method is planned? | Identify self-prep software, tax professional, employer assistance, free filing program, paper filing, or amended return route. |

| TAXSCP10 | What scope artifact should be produced? | Create tax prep brief, filing-year checklist, jurisdiction question list, or professional handoff plan. |
