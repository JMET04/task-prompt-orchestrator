# Professional Workflow Personal Reputation Online Presence Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal reputation, online presence, search results, social profiles, public criticism, reviews, misinformation, impersonation, professional biography, portfolio trust, media requests, privacy cleanup, evidence collection, reputation recovery, or long-term credibility building. This matrix supports organization, communication, and documentation; it does not replace legal advice, crisis communications counsel, platform enforcement, cybersecurity incident response, mental-health crisis care, or public-relations representation.

## Personal Reputation Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| PRSCOPE01 | What reputation or online-presence issue is in scope? | Define search result, social profile, review, accusation, press mention, impersonation, old content, portfolio, or public criticism. |
| PRSCOPE02 | Who is affected? | Identify individual, creator, student, job seeker, professional, founder, volunteer, public figure, or family member. |
| PRSCOPE03 | What outcome is realistic? | Choose clarity, cleanup, response, documentation, de-escalation, profile refresh, evidence packet, or recovery plan. |
| PRSCOPE04 | What audience matters? | Map employers, clients, peers, school, community, customers, followers, journalists, family, or searchers. |
| PRSCOPE05 | What harm or risk level exists? | Assess annoyance, reputational friction, employment risk, harassment, doxxing, legal threat, fraud, or safety concern. |
| PRSCOPE06 | What professional boundary applies? | Route defamation, threats, extortion, workplace discipline, legal notices, or press crisis to qualified support. |
| PRSCOPE07 | What privacy should be protected? | Protect address, phone, family, minors, health, finances, identity documents, location, and sensitive affiliations. |
| PRSCOPE08 | What source authority matters? | Distinguish official records, user-controlled profiles, third-party posts, news, reviews, forums, and copied content. |
| PRSCOPE09 | What response posture fits? | Choose no response, private correction, public statement, platform report, legal consult, or credibility-building path. |
| PRSCOPE10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce reputation brief, audience map, risk note, action boundary, or response posture. |

## Search And Presence Audit

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| REPAUD01 | What public surfaces should be audited? | Search name, aliases, usernames, email, phone, images, portfolio, social profiles, directories, and cached pages. |
| REPAUD02 | What search terms should be checked? | Combine name with city, employer, school, profession, project, allegation, old username, or common misspelling. |
| REPAUD03 | What result type is found? | Classify profile, article, review, database, image, forum, archived page, public record, video, or impersonation. |
| REPAUD04 | What visibility priority applies? | Rank by search page, platform reach, audience relevance, recency, credibility, and harm potential. |
| REPAUD05 | What is user-controlled? | Identify profiles, websites, bios, posts, portfolio pages, domain records, and settings the user can directly edit. |
| REPAUD06 | What is third-party controlled? | Identify media, forums, reviews, directories, data brokers, court/public records, and platform-hosted content. |
| REPAUD07 | What evidence should be captured? | Save URLs, screenshots, dates, snippets, author, platform, ranking, and access notes. |
| REPAUD08 | What should not be amplified? | Avoid sharing harmful links unnecessarily, repeated public searches from logged-in accounts, or reactive posting. |
| REPAUD09 | What refresh cadence applies? | Recheck after cleanup, job search, launch, press, conflict, legal update, or monthly monitoring. |
| REPAUD10 | What audit artifact is required? | Produce search audit, visibility map, action list, evidence folder, or monitoring checklist. |

| PROOFP08 | What format is appropriate? | Choose PDF, folder, timeline, statement, table, archive, or secure share. |

| PROOFP10 | What proof artifact is required? | Produce evidence packet, timeline, fact table, redacted folder, or credibility brief. |
