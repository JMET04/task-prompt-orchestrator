# Professional Workflow Food Meal Grocery Nutrition Kitchen Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must manage meals, groceries, pantry inventory, cooking prep, household food routines, basic nutrition preferences, dietary restrictions, food safety organization, leftovers, kitchen operations, meal budgets, or casual hosting food logistics.

This supports organization, planning, communication, and safe food-handling workflows. It does not replace medical nutrition therapy, dietitian care, allergy treatment, eating-disorder care, clinical advice, food-safety regulators, restaurant licensing, or emergency medical services.

## Food And Meal Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| FDSCOPE01 | What food workflow is needed? | Classify meal plan, grocery run, pantry cleanup, cooking prep, dietary coordination, leftovers, food safety, hosting, or kitchen routine. |
| FDSCOPE02 | Who will eat the food? | Identify household members, guests, children, elders, caregivers, athletes, workers, patients, or people with restrictions. |
| FDSCOPE03 | What meal period or event applies? | Define breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, school lunch, work meal, travel food, batch cook, party, or holiday meal. |
| FDSCOPE04 | What constraints shape the food plan? | Capture budget, time, equipment, skill, storage, culture, religion, dietary needs, allergies, and availability. |
| FDSCOPE05 | What safety or health boundary exists? | Note allergies, pregnancy, immune risk, choking risk, spoilage, eating-disorder concern, or clinician-directed diet. |
| FDSCOPE06 | What source should guide decisions? | Use household preferences, official food-safety guidance, product labels, clinician instructions, or trusted recipes. |
| FDSCOPE07 | What outcome is expected? | Define fed household, reduced waste, safer storage, lower cost, better variety, guest-ready menu, or routine stability. |
| FDSCOPE08 | What should not be optimized alone? | Avoid focusing only on calories, cost, speed, novelty, or aesthetics when safety, access, and wellbeing matter. |
| FDSCOPE09 | What handoff is needed? | Coordinate shopper, cook, caregiver, guest, school, workplace, delivery service, or cleanup owner. |
| FDSCOPE10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce meal brief, food constraints list, household preference map, or kitchen workflow plan. |
