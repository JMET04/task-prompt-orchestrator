# Professional Workflow Personal Productivity Life Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal productivity, life administration, goals, priorities, time planning, task systems, focus, habits, personal knowledge, non-advisory budgeting, wellness coordination, relationships, digital organization, or personal review routines.

## Personal Life Scope And Operating Context

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| LIFESCOPE01 | What life area is in scope? | Define work, study, family, health routines, money admin, home, relationships, learning, creative work, or personal systems. |
| LIFESCOPE02 | What outcome does the person want? | State concrete desired change, deadline, success signal, constraint, emotional need, and acceptable tradeoff. |
| LIFESCOPE03 | What current friction exists? | Identify overload, procrastination, unclear priorities, missing information, scattered tools, low energy, conflict, debt admin, or decision paralysis. |
| LIFESCOPE04 | What role or season matters? | Capture student, parent, caregiver, employee, founder, job seeker, retiree, recovery period, transition, move, or crisis season. |
| LIFESCOPE05 | What capacity is realistic? | Estimate time, attention, money, support, energy, mobility, tools, childcare, and recovery needs. |
| LIFESCOPE06 | What boundaries must be respected? | Respect privacy, autonomy, safety, medical/legal/financial advice limits, relationship boundaries, and cultural context. |
| LIFESCOPE07 | What tools are already used? | Inventory calendar, notes, task app, email, files, finance app, habit tracker, paper notebook, and communication channels. |
| LIFESCOPE08 | What must not be optimized away? | Preserve rest, relationships, health appointments, values, accessibility, joy, long-term trust, and recovery time. |
| LIFESCOPE09 | What proof of progress is useful? | Define completed tasks, reduced backlog, routine adherence, decision made, document filed, conflict resolved, or calmer week. |
| LIFESCOPE10 | What is out of scope? | Exclude diagnosis, therapy, legal advice, investment advice, tax advice, medical treatment, and coercive control of other people. |
