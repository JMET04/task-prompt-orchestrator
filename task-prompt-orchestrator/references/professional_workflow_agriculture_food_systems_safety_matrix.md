# Professional Workflow Agriculture Food Systems Safety Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle agriculture, farm operations, livestock, crops, food production, food processing, food safety, labeling, agribusiness, farm-to-table supply chains, cold chain, traceability, recalls, sustainability, or agricultural market access.

## Agriculture Food Scope And Product Definition

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| AGRSCOPE01 | What agriculture or food workflow is involved? | Define crop production, livestock, aquaculture, farm operations, processing, packaging, distribution, retail, food service, compliance, or recall. |
| AGRSCOPE02 | What product or commodity is involved? | Identify produce, grain, dairy, meat, poultry, seafood, beverage, packaged food, ingredient, feed, seed, fiber, or specialty crop. |
| AGRSCOPE03 | Who are the stakeholders? | Map growers, workers, processors, packers, distributors, retailers, restaurants, consumers, certifiers, regulators, buyers, and communities. |
| AGRSCOPE04 | What lifecycle stage applies? | Classify planning, planting, growing, harvest, postharvest, processing, packaging, storage, distribution, sale, consumption, or recall. |
| AGRSCOPE05 | What safety or quality outcome matters? | Define food safety, yield, animal welfare, quality grade, shelf life, traceability, sustainability, cost, market access, or consumer trust. |
| AGRSCOPE06 | What geography and season apply? | Record region, climate, soil, water source, pest pressure, harvest window, market season, and local regulation. |
| AGRSCOPE07 | What regulatory or certification boundary applies? | Identify food safety law, organic, GAP, HACCP, FSMA-like rules, animal health, pesticide, import/export, halal/kosher, or buyer standard. |
| AGRSCOPE08 | What vulnerable population or public health risk exists? | Flag allergens, pathogens, infants, elderly consumers, immunocompromised consumers, worker safety, and animal disease. |
| AGRSCOPE09 | What records must be created? | Define farm log, input log, harvest record, lot code, COA, batch record, sanitation log, temperature log, and distribution record. |
| AGRSCOPE10 | What is out of scope? | Exclude veterinary diagnosis, pesticide application instructions without label authority, medical nutrition therapy, or unsupported health claims. |
