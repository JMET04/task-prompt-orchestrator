# Professional Workflow Content Operations Editorial Distribution Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must plan, create, edit, approve, publish, distribute, localize, moderate, measure, archive, repurpose, or govern content across blogs, newsletters, social posts, videos, podcasts, documentation, community posts, knowledge bases, campaigns, creator workflows, or editorial programs.

## Content Operations Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| CNTSCOPE01 | What content program is in scope? | Define blog, newsletter, social, video, podcast, documentation, help center, campaign, community, editorial, or creator program. |
| CNTSCOPE02 | What content outcome matters most? | Choose awareness, education, trust, conversion, retention, support deflection, community growth, thought leadership, or search traffic. |
| CNTSCOPE03 | What audience is the content for? | Identify persona, buyer stage, user maturity, language, region, accessibility need, channel behavior, and intent. |
| CNTSCOPE04 | What brand or editorial voice applies? | Capture tone, style, vocabulary, claims boundary, visual identity, taboo topics, and cultural fit. |
| CNTSCOPE05 | What content format is required? | Choose article, guide, prompt, template, landing page, post, thread, short video, webinar, podcast, infographic, or FAQ. |
| CNTSCOPE06 | What channel or platform constraints apply? | Account for length, aspect ratio, algorithm, metadata, captions, links, hashtags, embeds, comments, and scheduling rules. |
| CNTSCOPE07 | What source or evidence standard applies? | Define citations, interviews, data, screenshots, product facts, legal proof, expert review, or firsthand examples. |
| CNTSCOPE08 | What compliance or sensitivity boundary exists? | Check copyright, claims, privacy, regulated advice, minors, medical/legal/finance, brand risk, and moderation exposure. |
| CNTSCOPE09 | What content artifact is required? | Produce content brief, editorial calendar, draft, media pack, approval packet, publishing checklist, or performance report. |
| CNTSCOPE10 | What evidence proves content operations worked? | Use reach, engagement, qualified traffic, conversion, retention, support reduction, community activity, and learning evidence. |
