# Professional Workflow Transportation Mobility Fleet Operations Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must plan, operate, monitor, improve, or govern passenger mobility, public transport, freight transport, fleet operations, routing, dispatch, terminals, drivers, vehicles, ticketing, safety, compliance, and transport performance.

## Transportation Mobility Scope

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| MOBILSCOPE01 | What transportation or mobility workflow is involved? | Define public transit, shuttle, rideshare, taxi, delivery fleet, trucking, rail, maritime, aviation support, micromobility, or logistics transport context. |
| MOBILSCOPE02 | Who are the users or stakeholders? | Identify passengers, riders, drivers, dispatchers, carriers, shippers, consignees, fleet managers, regulators, agencies, and communities. |
| MOBILSCOPE03 | What service objective matters most? | Define safety, reliability, coverage, speed, accessibility, cost, utilization, emissions, customer experience, or compliance goal. |
| MOBILSCOPE04 | What geography and network apply? | Map service area, corridors, hubs, depots, terminals, routes, stops, zones, lanes, geofences, and jurisdiction boundaries. |
| MOBILSCOPE05 | What assets or capacity are involved? | Capture vehicles, drivers, crews, chargers, fuel, docks, terminals, yards, slots, gates, loading bays, and maintenance capacity. |
| MOBILSCOPE06 | What demand pattern must be served? | Identify peak/off-peak, seasonality, events, school/work trips, freight waves, delivery windows, and emergency demand. |
| MOBILSCOPE07 | What service promise applies? | Define headway, pickup ETA, delivery window, on-time target, accessibility, comfort, tracking, price, and support expectations. |
| MOBILSCOPE08 | What systems or data are involved? | Identify TMS, dispatch, telematics, AVL, ticketing, fare, routing, WMS, OMS, ELD, CMMS, CRM, and incident systems. |
| MOBILSCOPE09 | What safety or regulatory boundary applies? | Check driver hours, vehicle inspection, traffic rules, transit regulations, hazardous materials, accessibility, insurance, and data privacy. |
| MOBILSCOPE10 | What transport artifact is required? | Produce route plan, dispatch plan, fleet plan, safety case, incident report, schedule, terminal plan, dashboard, or improvement roadmap. |
