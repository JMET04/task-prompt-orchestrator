# Professional Workflow Home Inventory Possessions Warranty Manuals Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle household possessions, item inventory, valuables, serial numbers, model numbers, receipts, warranties, manuals, maintenance records, loaned or borrowed items, insurance-ready inventory packets, moving inventory, restocking, disposal, donation, or recurring inventory review.

Use this for personal and household organization, evidence, and maintenance records. Do not use it to replace insurance coverage decisions, legal or tax advice, professional appraisal, hazardous disposal authority, or cybersecurity incident response.
