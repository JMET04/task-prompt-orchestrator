# Professional Workflow Personal Civic Identity Records Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal identity documents, passports, driver's licenses, civil records, name or address changes, government benefit applications, citizen-service appointments, permits, personal notices, official forms, notarization/apostille, translations, document renewals, lost-document recovery, or official-record organization. This matrix supports administrative organization and official-source checklists; it does not provide legal advice, immigration advice, benefits eligibility determinations, or authority to submit official forms for the user.

|---|---|---|
| CIVSCOPE01 | What civic or identity task is in scope? | Define ID, passport, license, civil record, name change, address update, benefit, permit, notice, form, appointment, or records archive. |

| CIVSCOPE05 | What deadline or urgency exists? | Track expiration, appointment date, travel date, response deadline, hearing, enrollment, benefit window, or penalty risk. |
| CIVSCOPE06 | What proof of identity or authority is needed? | Organize ID, birth record, citizenship/residency proof, address proof, relationship proof, authorization, and signatures. |

| CIVSCOPE10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce civic task brief, official-source checklist, deadline map, privacy note, or professional-handoff question list. |
