# Professional Workflow Local Services Home Family Operations Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle local services, household operations, home maintenance, repairs, contractors, utilities, moving, errands, family logistics, home inventory, domestic safety, household budgets, or recurring home routines.

## Local Home Operations Scope

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| HOMEOPSCOPE01 | What home or local-life need is in scope? | Define repair, maintenance, cleaning, moving, utility, errand, family logistics, home safety, vendor visit, or household admin task. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE02 | What property or household context matters? | Capture renter/owner status, apartment/house, building rules, landlord/HOA, occupants, pets, accessibility, and neighborhood constraints. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE03 | Who is affected by the task? | Identify household members, landlord, neighbors, vendors, guests, children, older adults, disabled people, pets, and shared-space users. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE04 | What urgency applies? | Classify emergency, same-day, scheduled, seasonal, preventive, convenience, aesthetic, or long-term improvement. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE05 | What outcome proves success? | Define fixed issue, safe condition, completed appointment, clean area, moved item, paid bill, stored document, or reduced recurring friction. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE06 | What constraints shape the plan? | Capture budget, schedule, access, noise rules, building approvals, weather, tools, transport, warranty, and safety limits. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE07 | What safety boundary applies? | Avoid unsafe electrical, gas, structural, medical, legal, or security actions; route high-risk tasks to qualified professionals. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE08 | What information is already available? | Gather photos, receipts, manuals, lease, warranty, utility bills, vendor quotes, measurements, and prior service records. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE09 | What decision must be made? | Decide DIY versus professional, repair versus replace, schedule now versus defer, vendor choice, budget limit, or escalation path. |
| HOMEOPSCOPE10 | What household artifact is required? | Produce task brief, vendor request, checklist, schedule, inventory, budget tracker, or maintenance log. |
