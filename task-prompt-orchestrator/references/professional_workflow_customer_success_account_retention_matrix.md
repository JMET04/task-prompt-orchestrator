# Professional Workflow Customer Success Account Retention Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must manage post-sale customer success, account ownership, implementation, activation, adoption, value realization, support escalation, account health, renewal, expansion, advocacy, churn prevention, customer governance, CRM hygiene, or recurring-revenue learning.

## Customer Success Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| CSSCOPE01 | What customer relationship is in scope? | Define account, user group, client, subscriber, tenant, partner account, enterprise customer, cohort, or managed service client. |
| CSSCOPE02 | What post-sale outcome matters most? | Choose activation, adoption, value, retention, renewal, expansion, satisfaction, risk reduction, support quality, or advocacy. |
| CSSCOPE03 | What product or service commitment exists? | Capture subscription, contract, SLA, statement of work, pilot, onboarding package, managed service, or support entitlement. |
| CSSCOPE04 | What lifecycle stage is current? | Classify handoff, onboarding, implementation, active use, value review, renewal, expansion, rescue, churn, or win-back. |
| CSSCOPE05 | What customer segment changes the motion? | Distinguish self-serve, SMB, mid-market, enterprise, strategic, regulated, partner-led, high-touch, pooled, or digital CS. |
| CSSCOPE06 | What customer promise must be kept? | Link sales claims, success criteria, ROI promise, service level, migration scope, support response, or compliance commitment. |
| CSSCOPE07 | What internal owner is accountable? | Assign customer success manager, account manager, implementation lead, support owner, sales owner, product owner, or executive sponsor. |
| CSSCOPE08 | What customer-side owner is needed? | Identify champion, admin, business owner, technical owner, finance/procurement, executive sponsor, user lead, or blocker. |
| CSSCOPE09 | What account artifact is required? | Produce account plan, success plan, onboarding plan, health report, renewal plan, expansion plan, escalation brief, or QBR deck. |
| CSSCOPE10 | What proof shows customer success worked? | Use adoption, usage, value metric, NPS/CSAT, renewal, expansion, case study, support trend, and customer quote evidence. |
