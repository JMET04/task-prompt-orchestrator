# Professional Workflow Personal Estate Planning Wills Directives Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal estate planning preparation, wills, trusts, beneficiary records, powers of attorney, advance directives, healthcare proxies, guardianship preferences, executor or fiduciary information, digital legacy, probate or tax question packets, estate document packets, or recurring estate review.

Use this for personal organization, records, question lists, trusted-person handoffs, and professional-prep packets. Do not use it to provide legal, tax, investment, fiduciary, probate, guardianship, healthcare, end-of-life, or estate-planning advice, and do not replace an attorney, tax professional, financial advisor, licensed fiduciary, healthcare professional, court, government agency, or official form source.

## ESTSCP - Estate Planning Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| ESTSCP01 | What planning situation is being organized? | Define first-time planning, life-event update, document refresh, move, family change, asset change, illness, eldercare, or post-death packet preparation. |
| ESTSCP02 | Who is included in the planning context? | Capture individual, spouse or partner, children, dependents, parents, blended family, pets, business partners, or trusted helpers. |
| ESTSCP03 | What jurisdiction or residence questions exist? | Flag home state, prior residence, cross-border assets, nonresident dependents, or relocation questions for qualified review. |
| ESTSCP04 | What documents already exist? | Inventory wills, codicils, trusts, POAs, healthcare directives, beneficiary forms, deeds, titles, letters, and prior attorney files. |
| ESTSCP05 | What documents are missing or outdated? | Identify absent signatures, obsolete agents, old addresses, changed assets, deceased beneficiaries, or documents stored in unknown places. |
| ESTSCP06 | What privacy boundary applies? | Protect identity data, account numbers, health information, family conflict details, asset values, login credentials, and unsigned drafts. |
| ESTSCP07 | What urgency level applies? | Triage routine review, upcoming travel, surgery, serious illness, incapacity risk, family conflict, recent death, or court deadline. |
| ESTSCP08 | What professionals may need handoff? | List attorney, tax professional, financial advisor, insurance agent, fiduciary, healthcare provider, notary, or court contact. |
| ESTSCP09 | What decisions should not be made here? | Do not choose legal instruments, tax strategies, medical choices, fiduciary duties, guardians, or probate actions without qualified review. |
| ESTSCP10 | What scope artifact should be produced? | Create estate planning brief, document inventory, question list, trusted-person map, or professional handoff packet. |
