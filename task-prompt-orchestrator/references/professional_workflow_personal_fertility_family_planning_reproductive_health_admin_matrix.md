# Professional Workflow Personal Fertility Family Planning Reproductive Health Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must manage personal fertility, family planning, preconception preparation, trying-to-conceive administration, reproductive health appointments, contraception or birth-control records, cycle tracking for care discussions, fertility evaluation logistics, IVF or IUI clinic administration, reproductive labs and imaging follow-up, clinician-instructed medication support, fertility insurance and billing records, cryopreservation or donor-record administration, adoption or surrogacy document preparation, or recurring family-building review.

This supports personal organization, scheduling, records, reminders, communication drafts, question lists, and professional handoff preparation. It does not provide medical, fertility, contraception, pregnancy, genetic, embryology, legal, ethical, religious, financial, insurance, employment, adoption, surrogacy, or mental-health advice; decide treatment options; interpret results; determine eligibility; or replace clinicians, fertility specialists, pharmacists, genetic counselors, lawyers, agencies, insurers, employers, clergy, ethics boards, or emergency services.
