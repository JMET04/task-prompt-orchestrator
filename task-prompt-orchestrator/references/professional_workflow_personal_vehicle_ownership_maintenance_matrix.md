# Professional Workflow Personal Vehicle Ownership Maintenance Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal vehicle purchase, ownership, maintenance, repair, inspections, registration coordination, insurance administration, roadside incidents, accident documentation, fuel/charging, commuting, seasonal readiness, vehicle technology, resale, or total cost of ownership. This matrix supports organization and service coordination; it does not replace licensed mechanic diagnosis, legal advice, insurance coverage decisions, emergency services, or official traffic and vehicle regulations.

## Personal Vehicle Scope And Safety Boundary

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| VEHCSCOPE01 | What vehicle context is in scope? | Define car, motorcycle, scooter, RV, trailer, bicycle-adjacent motor vehicle, EV, hybrid, shared household vehicle, or leased vehicle. |
| VEHCSCOPE02 | What ownership stage applies? | Identify shopping, purchase, financing, registration, daily use, maintenance, repair, incident, renewal, sale, or disposal. |
| VEHCSCOPE03 | Who uses or manages the vehicle? | Identify owner, driver, family member, teen driver, caregiver, fleet-like household, mechanic, dealer, insurer, lender, or buyer. |
| VEHCSCOPE04 | What safety boundary applies? | Route crashes, injury, fire, severe malfunction, carbon monoxide, brake/steering issues, or unsafe driving conditions to emergency/professional help. |
| VEHCSCOPE05 | What records are available? | Gather title, registration, insurance, inspection, service history, warranty, loan/lease, purchase documents, and manuals. |
| VEHCSCOPE06 | What legal or regulatory boundary applies? | Use official sources for registration, inspection, licensing, emissions, parking, tolls, and traffic rules; avoid legal advice. |
| VEHCSCOPE07 | What financial boundary applies? | Organize costs and options without recommending loans, insurance coverage, or legal/financial decisions. |
| VEHCSCOPE08 | What privacy data should be protected? | Redact VIN when unnecessary, address, license plate, driver's license, loan details, telematics, and location history. |
| VEHCSCOPE09 | What usage pattern matters? | Capture commute, family, rideshare/delivery, road trips, winter driving, off-road, towing, city parking, or low-mileage use. |
| VEHCSCOPE10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce vehicle profile, ownership task brief, safety boundary, record checklist, or service coordination plan. |
