# Professional Workflow Market Channel Sales Operations Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must take an offer, product, service, campaign, prompt library, app, or professional deliverable to market through target segments, channels, demand generation, sales motion, partner routes, CRM operations, proposals, deal handoff, expansion, and revenue operations learning.

## Market And GTM Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| GTMSCOPE01 | What offer is being taken to market? | Define product, service, package, template, subscription, professional deliverable, campaign, or channel-ready asset. |
| GTMSCOPE02 | What market entry objective matters? | Choose awareness, demand, pipeline, revenue, distribution, retention, expansion, community growth, or category validation. |
| GTMSCOPE03 | What buyer or user problem anchors the motion? | Link GTM to pain, job-to-be-done, trigger event, urgency, and willingness to act. |
| GTMSCOPE04 | What market stage is current? | Classify discovery, positioning, launch, growth, optimization, relaunch, geographic expansion, or channel scale. |
| GTMSCOPE05 | What commercial model constrains GTM? | Account for free, paid, subscription, usage, services, enterprise, marketplace, reseller, or bundled model. |
| GTMSCOPE06 | What route-to-market options exist? | Compare direct, self-serve, sales-led, partner-led, marketplace, community, content-led, events, or outbound routes. |
| GTMSCOPE07 | What geographic or language scope applies? | Define territories, localization, legal constraints, cultural fit, pricing region, and channel availability. |
| GTMSCOPE08 | What sales cycle length and decision complexity apply? | Distinguish impulse, transactional, consultative, committee, procurement-led, or regulated buying. |
| GTMSCOPE09 | What GTM owner is accountable? | Assign marketing, sales, product, founder, partnerships, customer success, RevOps, or channel owner. |
| GTMSCOPE10 | What GTM artifact is required? | Produce GTM brief, ICP map, positioning, channel plan, campaign calendar, sales playbook, pipeline report, or launch review. |
