# Professional Workflow Creator Personal Brand Knowledge Monetization Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle creator strategy, personal brand, audience building, content pillars, owned channels, newsletters, courses, digital products, paid communities, sponsorships, affiliates, launches, creator operations, monetization, risk, and performance learning.

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| CREATORSCOPE01 | What creator business is being built? | Define solo creator, educator, consultant, influencer, newsletter, video channel, podcast, course brand, community, or niche media business. |
| CREATORSCOPE02 | What audience problem is being served? | Identify audience, pain, aspiration, job-to-be-done, context, willingness to pay, and trust barrier. |
| CREATORSCOPE03 | What creator advantage exists? | Capture expertise, story, taste, access, proof, method, personality, community, distribution, or credibility edge. |
| CREATORSCOPE04 | What monetization stage applies? | Classify exploration, audience building, first offer, launch, recurring revenue, sponsorship, licensing, or portfolio scaling. |
| CREATORSCOPE05 | What platform context matters? | Map social platforms, newsletter, podcast apps, website, community platform, course platform, marketplace, and payment tools. |
| CREATORSCOPE06 | What success metric matters now? | Choose reach, subscribers, engagement, leads, sales, retention, referrals, revenue, profit, trust, or creative consistency. |
| CREATORSCOPE07 | What constraints shape the work? | Capture time, budget, production capacity, platform risk, privacy, burnout, legal rights, audience trust, and family/work boundaries. |
| CREATORSCOPE08 | What ethical boundary applies? | Avoid misleading claims, fake scarcity, undisclosed sponsorship, exploitative community, unsafe advice, and unqualified professional claims. |
| CREATORSCOPE09 | What assets already exist? | Inventory posts, videos, essays, frameworks, slides, notes, client work, testimonials, audience data, offers, and email list. |
| CREATORSCOPE10 | What creator artifact is required? | Produce positioning brief, creator operating plan, offer map, content system, or monetization roadmap. |
