# Professional Workflow Energy Utilities Grid Water Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle energy, electric grid, water, wastewater, gas, district energy, utility customer service, utility assets, rate cases, reliability, outage response, demand programs, decarbonization, resilience, or regulated utility operations.

## Utility Scope And Service Mandate

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| UTILSCOPE01 | What utility service is involved? | Define electricity, gas, water, wastewater, stormwater, district energy, broadband utility, microgrid, or multi-utility scope. |
| UTILSCOPE02 | What service territory and customers apply? | Identify geography, customer classes, critical customers, vulnerable customers, industrial loads, public facilities, and growth areas. |
| UTILSCOPE03 | What regulatory or franchise obligation applies? | Record service obligation, tariff, commission rule, permit, consent decree, franchise agreement, reliability standard, or public-health rule. |
| UTILSCOPE04 | What operational objective is being optimized? | Define safety, reliability, affordability, resilience, decarbonization, water quality, customer service, compliance, or asset performance. |
| UTILSCOPE05 | What asset or system boundary is relevant? | Identify generation, transmission, distribution, substations, pipelines, treatment plants, pumps, meters, reservoirs, controls, or customer premise equipment. |
| UTILSCOPE06 | What planning horizon applies? | Set real-time operations, daily dispatch, seasonal planning, annual budget, rate cycle, capital plan, integrated resource plan, or long-term resilience. |
| UTILSCOPE07 | What public safety risk exists? | Assess electric shock, fire, gas leak, water contamination, flooding, confined space, pressure, traffic, cyber, and emergency response risk. |
| UTILSCOPE08 | What customer impact must be considered? | Capture outages, billing, rates, shutoffs, low-income support, language access, medical baseline, business interruption, and public notices. |
| UTILSCOPE09 | What data or telemetry is needed? | Identify SCADA, AMI, GIS, asset records, outage calls, hydraulic models, load forecasts, weather, lab tests, inspections, and work orders. |
| UTILSCOPE10 | What is out of scope? | Exclude unlicensed engineering signoff, unsafe switching instructions, unauthorized grid operations, unsupported rate advice, or emergency dispatch replacement. |
