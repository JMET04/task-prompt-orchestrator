# Professional Workflow Spiritual Religious Ritual Community Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle spiritual or religious practice, worship attendance, faith community participation, rituals, holidays, life-cycle ceremonies, interfaith family coordination, sacred-space etiquette, dietary or dress observance, volunteering, donations, spiritual study, community notices, conflict around belief practice, or reflection. This matrix supports planning and respectful coordination; it does not replace clergy, religious authorities, legal advice, medical advice, safeguarding, security, venue permits, or emergency services.

## Spiritual Religious Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| SPIRSCP01 | What spiritual or religious context is in scope? | Define faith tradition, spiritual practice, secular ritual, family custom, community event, study, holiday, or life-cycle ceremony. |
| SPIRSCP02 | Who is involved? | Identify individual, family, partner, child, elder, faith leader, congregation, guests, interfaith relatives, or community group. |
| SPIRSCP03 | What outcome is desired? | Choose practice plan, ceremony coordination, respectful participation, learning path, family alignment, volunteer plan, or reflection. |
| SPIRSCP04 | What authority boundary applies? | Route doctrine, ritual validity, clergy decisions, conversion, pastoral care, and sacramental questions to appropriate leaders. |
| SPIRSCP05 | What legal or safety boundary applies? | Route permits, discrimination, custody, employment, food safety, crowd safety, and medical accommodation to qualified support. |
| SPIRSCP06 | What privacy or sensitivity matters? | Protect beliefs, conversion status, family conflict, donations, health, immigration, sexuality, and minors. |
| SPIRSCP07 | What level of observance applies? | Capture strict, moderate, cultural, exploratory, mixed-faith, secular, private, or community-led practice. |
| SPIRSCP08 | What accommodation is needed? | Consider language, disability access, diet, dress, schedule, gendered space, sensory needs, and remote participation. |
| SPIRSCP09 | What respect rule should guide the workflow? | Avoid appropriation, mockery, forced participation, proselytizing pressure, and flattening diverse traditions. |
| SPIRSCP10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce spiritual practice brief, ritual coordination note, boundary map, participant profile, or respect checklist. |
