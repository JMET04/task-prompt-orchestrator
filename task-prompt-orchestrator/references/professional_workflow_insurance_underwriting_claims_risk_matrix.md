# Professional Workflow Insurance Underwriting Claims Risk Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle insurance products, applications, underwriting, risk assessment, policy issuance, endorsements, renewals, claims, loss investigation, adjustment, fraud controls, payments, subrogation, reinsurance, policyholder service, regulatory compliance, or insurance data operations.

|---|---|---|

| INSSCOPE05 | What jurisdiction and regulatory boundary applies? | Record state/country, admitted/non-admitted status, filing rules, claims handling deadlines, privacy, producer licensing, and consumer notices. |
| INSSCOPE06 | What risk or coverage question is being answered? | Separate eligibility, pricing, coverage interpretation, claim causation, liability, damages, fraud, reserves, or recovery question. |
