# Professional Workflow Gifts Holidays Social Etiquette Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must manage gifts, holidays, birthdays, weddings, condolence gestures, thank-you notes, hosting gifts, social etiquette, guest lists, group gifts, gift budgets, delivery, wrapping, or relationship-sensitive social gestures.

This supports organization, communication, planning, and cultural sensitivity. It does not replace legal advice, tax advice, religious authority, cultural authority, therapy, mediation, or professional event planning.

## Gift And Social Gesture Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| GIFTSCP01 | What social gesture is needed? | Classify gift, card, condolence, celebration, hosting contribution, thank-you, apology, group gift, or holiday plan. |
| GIFTSCP02 | Who is giving and receiving? | Identify giver, recipient, family, colleague, client, host, child, elder, group, or institution. |
| GIFTSCP03 | What relationship context matters? | Note closeness, formality, history, hierarchy, boundaries, culture, religion, workplace policy, and sensitivity. |
| GIFTSCP04 | What occasion drives the gesture? | Capture birthday, wedding, baby, graduation, holiday, illness, bereavement, housewarming, promotion, or visit. |
| GIFTSCP05 | What outcome is intended? | Define appreciation, celebration, comfort, apology, reciprocity, inclusion, respect, encouragement, or hospitality. |
| GIFTSCP06 | What budget boundary applies? | Set amount, group contribution, shipping, wrapping, card, tax/customs, and affordability. |
| GIFTSCP07 | What policy or etiquette boundary applies? | Check workplace gift rules, school rules, anti-bribery policy, religious norms, cultural expectations, and family agreements. |
| GIFTSCP08 | What risk should be avoided? | Avoid pressure, inappropriate intimacy, dietary conflict, unsafe items, unequal treatment, clutter, or public embarrassment. |
| GIFTSCP09 | What timeline matters? | Track occasion date, shipping cutoff, RSVP, personalization time, handmade time, and thank-you deadline. |
| GIFTSCP10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce gesture brief, occasion tracker, budget note, or relationship-sensitive checklist. |
