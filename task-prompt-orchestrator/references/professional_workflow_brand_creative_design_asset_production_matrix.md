# Professional Workflow Brand Creative Design Asset Production Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must plan, brief, create, generate, review, adapt, publish, or maintain brand, creative, visual design, campaign creative, image/video assets, design systems, art direction, social/ecommerce ads, presentation visuals, UI visual concepts, packaging graphics, or reusable creative asset libraries.

## Brand Strategy And Creative Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| BRANDSC01 | What brand, product, offer, or initiative is in scope? | Define company, product line, campaign, feature, event, channel, market, or asset family boundary. |
| BRANDSC02 | What brand promise must the creative express? | Preserve positioning, value proposition, personality, trust cue, product truth, and customer expectation. |
| BRANDSC03 | What audience should the creative move? | Identify buyer, user, viewer, stakeholder, segment, maturity, culture, language, and accessibility need. |
| BRANDSC04 | What business outcome should the creative support? | Choose awareness, consideration, conversion, retention, education, launch, trust, internal alignment, or sales enablement. |
| BRANDSC05 | What brand rules are binding? | Capture logo, color, typography, tone, imagery, claims, accessibility, legal, channel, and partner co-branding rules. |
| BRANDSC06 | What creative freedom is allowed? | Define safe, exploratory, disruptive, premium, playful, technical, editorial, cinematic, minimalist, or experimental range. |
| BRANDSC07 | What must the creative avoid? | Exclude off-brand tone, misleading claims, unsafe imagery, overused tropes, competitor similarity, stereotypes, or clutter. |
| BRANDSC08 | What stakeholder owns brand approval? | Assign brand, marketing, product, legal, compliance, design lead, client, or executive approver. |
| BRANDSC09 | What source assets or references are available? | Inventory logos, fonts, product images, CAD files, style guides, prior campaigns, moodboards, and examples. |
| BRANDSC10 | What brand-scope artifact is required? | Produce creative scope note, brand constraint sheet, asset inventory, stakeholder map, or approval boundary. |
