# Professional Workflow Personal Substance Use Addiction Recovery Support Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must manage personal substance-use concerns, addiction recovery administration, sobriety support, alcohol or drug recovery records, treatment program coordination, detox or rehab logistics, outpatient program scheduling, support group attendance, sponsor or peer-support coordination, clinician-instructed medication-assisted treatment records, harm-reduction logistics, relapse-prevention materials, recovery privacy, family/support communication, workplace or school recovery documentation, or recurring recovery review.

This supports personal organization, scheduling, records, reminders, communication drafts, question lists, support maps, and professional handoff preparation. It does not diagnose addiction or intoxication, assess withdrawal or overdose risk, provide detox, medical, psychiatric, legal, employment, child-safety, crisis, or emergency advice, recommend medication or treatment level, guarantee recovery outcomes, or replace clinicians, therapists, addiction specialists, pharmacists, emergency services, crisis lines, legal counsel, employers, schools, courts, probation/parole, treatment programs, peer-support groups, or social services.
