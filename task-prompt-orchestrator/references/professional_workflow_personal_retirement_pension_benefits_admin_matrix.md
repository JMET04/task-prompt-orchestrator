# Professional Workflow Personal Retirement Pension Benefits Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal retirement administration, pension records, retirement accounts, employer retirement plans, IRA-like accounts, rollover coordination, retirement contribution records, withdrawal records, required minimum distribution reminders, Social Security or public retirement benefit administration, Medicare or retiree health enrollment milestones, annuity records, beneficiary updates, long-term care planning records, or recurring retirement admin review.

Use this for personal organization, official-source checklists, account inventory, deadline tracking, document gathering, communication drafts, tax-prep records, advisor question packets, and professional handoff preparation. Do not use it to provide investment advice, tax advice, legal advice, fiduciary advice, retirement-income strategy, benefit-claiming strategy, annuity recommendations, insurance advice, medical advice, or to replace financial planners, tax preparers, benefits counselors, plan administrators, government agencies, attorneys, insurers, or healthcare advisors.
