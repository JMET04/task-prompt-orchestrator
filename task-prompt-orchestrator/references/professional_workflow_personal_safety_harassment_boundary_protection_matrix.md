# Professional Workflow Personal Safety Harassment Boundary Protection Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal safety planning, harassment, stalking concerns, boundary protection, unwanted contact, coercive behavior, unsafe routines, digital safety, evidence organization, trusted support, reporting preparation, de-escalation, safe exit planning, or recovery after personal safety incidents. This matrix structures planning and documentation; it does not replace emergency services, legal advice, law enforcement, domestic-violence advocacy, mental-health crisis support, workplace HR, campus safety, or qualified victim-support services.

## Personal Safety Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| PSAFESCP01 | What personal safety concern is in scope? | Define unwanted contact, harassment, stalking concern, boundary violation, unsafe person, unsafe place, coercion, threat, or routine vulnerability. |
| PSAFESCP02 | Who is affected? | Identify user, child, dependent adult, colleague, student, roommate, partner, former partner, customer, public figure, or online persona. |
| PSAFESCP03 | What setting matters? | Capture home, workplace, school, campus, public space, transit, event, online platform, shared housing, or travel route. |
| PSAFESCP04 | What immediate danger boundary applies? | Route imminent harm, weapons, violence, self-harm, active stalking, missing person, or emergency to local emergency services. |
| PSAFESCP05 | What authority or relationship context matters? | Note workplace, school, family, landlord, customer, client, platform, legal order, or caregiving relationship. |
| PSAFESCP06 | What privacy constraint exists? | Protect location, contact details, identity documents, minors, legal records, health data, and support contacts. |
| PSAFESCP07 | What goal is realistic? | Choose safety plan, documentation, boundary script, support network, reporting packet, digital lock-down, or routine change. |
| PSAFESCP08 | What professional support may be needed? | Identify emergency services, advocate, attorney, HR, campus safety, platform trust team, counselor, or social services. |
| PSAFESCP09 | What should not be handled by prompting alone? | Avoid legal strategy, confrontation, investigation, therapy, retaliation, surveillance, or safety-critical decisions without experts. |
| PSAFESCP10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce safety brief, concern map, escalation boundary, support list, or planning charter. |

## Boundary Planning And Contact Limits

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| BOUNDPRO01 | What boundary needs protection? | Define contact, access, time, location, physical space, emotional labor, money, work channel, or online interaction boundary. |

| BOUNDPRO03 | What communication should be minimal? | Draft short, clear, non-escalating language that states boundary, channel, and consequence without debate. |
| BOUNDPRO04 | What contact should be avoided? | Identify no-response cases, unsafe confrontation, private meetings, argument loops, late-night replies, or unrecorded calls. |
| BOUNDPRO05 | What third-party boundary is needed? | Set instructions for friends, family, colleagues, reception, school, landlord, or moderators not to share information. |

| BOUNDPRO08 | What safety tradeoff should be considered? | Balance blocking, documenting, notifying others, relocation, privacy, and risk of escalation. |
| BOUNDPRO09 | What boundary violation response is planned? | Choose record, do not engage, notify support, report, change routine, or seek professional help. |
| BOUNDPRO10 | What boundary artifact is required? | Produce boundary script, contact rule, third-party instruction, violation response plan, or boundary log. |

| HARRDOC08 | What privacy protection is needed? | Redact minors, addresses, unrelated personal data, medical details, and sensitive third-party information. |
| HARRDOC09 | What reporting packet should be prepared? | Assemble summary, timeline, evidence index, impact statement, requested action, and contact preferences. |
| HARRDOC10 | What documentation artifact is required? | Produce incident log, evidence index, timeline, report packet, or redacted summary. |
