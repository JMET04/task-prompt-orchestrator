# Professional Workflow Legal Services Matter Casework Dispute Resolution Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle legal services, legal matter intake, conflict checks, client communication, case facts, legal research coordination, document drafting, filings, discovery, negotiations, litigation support, dispute resolution, matter deadlines, billing, ethics, privilege, or matter closeout. This matrix structures legal work but does not provide legal advice.

## Legal Matter Intake And Scope

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| LSMATTER01 | What legal matter is being opened? | Define practice area, client, opposing parties, jurisdiction, issue type, urgency, desired outcome, and current procedural posture. |
| LSMATTER02 | Who is the client? | Identify individual, company, board, insured, beneficiary, fiduciary, family member, department, or entity representative. |
| LSMATTER03 | What authority to act exists? | Confirm engagement, representative capacity, signatory, power of attorney, board authorization, insurer authorization, or referral boundary. |
| LSMATTER04 | What deadlines or triggers are known? | Capture statute, filing deadline, hearing, response date, contract notice, appeal date, limitation period, and preservation duty. |
| LSMATTER05 | What facts are known at intake? | Record timeline, parties, documents, communications, witnesses, damages, prior advice, filings, and immediate risks. |
| LSMATTER06 | What client objective should be clarified? | Distinguish legal remedy, business goal, cost goal, timing goal, privacy concern, relationship goal, and risk tolerance. |
| LSMATTER07 | What services are in scope? | Define advice, drafting, negotiation, filing, litigation, investigation, compliance, due diligence, mediation, or limited-scope assistance. |
| LSMATTER08 | What services are out of scope? | Exclude unengaged parties, other jurisdictions, tax, immigration, criminal, regulatory, financial, or business advice unless assigned. |
| LSMATTER09 | What immediate protective action is needed? | Flag preservation notice, deadline hold, emergency filing, injunction, response extension, confidentiality step, or safety escalation. |
| LSMATTER10 | What intake artifact must be created? | Create matter summary, party list, deadline list, document checklist, conflict request, engagement path, and next-action owner. |
