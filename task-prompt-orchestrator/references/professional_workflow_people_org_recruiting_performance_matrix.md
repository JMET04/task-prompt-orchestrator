# Professional Workflow People Org Recruiting Performance Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow depends on organization design, team structure, staffing, job definitions, hiring, candidate assessment, onboarding, workforce capacity, scheduling, workload balance, performance goals, incentives, employee relations, wellbeing, offboarding, succession, or organization health.

## Org And People Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| ORGSCOPE01 | What organization or team is in scope? | Define company, department, squad, project team, shift, location, vendor team, or temporary working group. |
| ORGSCOPE02 | What people outcome matters most? | Choose capacity, speed, quality, coverage, retention, engagement, compliance, learning, safety, or accountability. |
| ORGSCOPE03 | What operating context shapes the people workflow? | Account for startup, enterprise, agency, factory, lab, service desk, retail, school, clinic, remote, hybrid, or field work. |
| ORGSCOPE04 | What current pain triggered the workflow? | Identify hiring gap, unclear ownership, overload, attrition, low performance, handoff failure, conflict, or scaling pressure. |
| ORGSCOPE05 | What people decisions will be made? | Decide roles, reporting lines, headcount, hiring, promotion, compensation, scheduling, coaching, transfer, or exit. |
| ORGSCOPE06 | What employment or engagement model applies? | Distinguish employee, contractor, agency, volunteer, intern, partner, franchise, union, gig, or AI-assisted labor model. |
| ORGSCOPE07 | What geography or legal context matters? | Capture country, state, labor rules, work authorization, language, local benefits, holidays, time zone, or works council needs. |
| ORGSCOPE08 | What confidentiality boundary applies? | Separate public org info, manager-only records, HR records, medical data, compensation, investigation notes, and legal holds. |
| ORGSCOPE09 | What people artifact is required? | Produce org brief, staffing plan, role charter, hiring plan, interview kit, onboarding plan, review cycle, or org health report. |
| ORGSCOPE10 | What success evidence should prove the people workflow worked? | Use fill rate, time to hire, ramp speed, performance, retention, coverage, satisfaction, fairness checks, and audit trail. |
