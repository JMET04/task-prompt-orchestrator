# Professional Workflow Personal Banking Credit Cards Debt Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal bank accounts, checking, savings, credit cards, debit cards, bill payments, autopay, debt tracking, loans, credit reports, fees, rates, bank communication, account security, or recurring personal banking review.

Use this for personal organization, reminders, recordkeeping, account inventory, payment coordination, dispute preparation, communication drafts, and professional handoff checklists. Do not use it to provide financial, investment, credit, debt-settlement, bankruptcy, tax, legal, lending, underwriting, fiduciary, or banking-regulatory advice, and do not replace a bank, credit bureau, lender, attorney, credit counselor, tax professional, or financial advisor.

## PBKSCP - Personal Banking Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| PBKSCP01 | What banking situation is being managed? | Define account inventory, bill payment, card admin, debt tracking, loan admin, credit report, fee issue, or security review. |
| PBKSCP02 | Which institution or account type is involved? | Identify bank, credit union, card issuer, lender, brokerage cash account, payment app, or credit bureau. |
| PBKSCP03 | Who owns or uses the account? | Capture individual, joint owner, authorized user, dependent, elder, household member, trustee, or business-use boundary. |
| PBKSCP04 | What financial boundary applies? | Organize facts and options without recommending borrowing, investing, payoff strategy, credit repair, bankruptcy, or legal action. |
| PBKSCP05 | What deadline or event matters? | Note statement close, due date, grace period, fee deadline, rate change, offer expiry, dispute window, or loan maturity. |
| PBKSCP06 | What official source should be used? | Use bank portal, card statement, loan servicer, credit bureau, written terms, notices, and verified support channels. |
| PBKSCP07 | What records need privacy protection? | Protect account numbers, card numbers, balances, tax IDs, logins, statements, credit reports, and payoff letters. |
| PBKSCP08 | What connected workflows matter? | Link to personal finance, taxes, fraud recovery, subscriptions, documents, housing, vehicle, insurance, or estate records. |
| PBKSCP09 | What success criteria define progress? | Set complete account list, on-time payment, filed dispute, fee resolved, records archived, or review completed. |
| PBKSCP10 | What scope artifact should be produced? | Create banking admin brief, account map, payment tracker, or issue-resolution checklist. |
