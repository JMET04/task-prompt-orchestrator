# Professional Workflow Travel Relocation Immigration Settlement Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle travel planning, international trips, visas, immigration document coordination, border entry preparation, lodging, ground transport, safety, packing, travel money, relocation, expat settlement, or post-trip review. This matrix organizes logistics and documents; it does not provide legal immigration advice.

## Travel Relocation Scope

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| TRIPSCOPE01 | What trip or relocation is being planned? | Define vacation, business trip, study, family visit, medical travel, move, remote work, immigration landing, or repatriation. |
| TRIPSCOPE02 | Who is traveling? | Capture travelers, ages, passports, accessibility needs, medical needs if user-provided, pets, dependents, and group roles. |
| TRIPSCOPE03 | What destination and route apply? | List origin, destination, transit countries, stops, dates, time zones, and border crossings. |
| TRIPSCOPE04 | What purpose and constraints matter? | Capture budget, purpose, work/study duties, visa limits, school calendar, health, safety, language, and flexibility. |
| TRIPSCOPE05 | What risk level applies? | Consider destination advisories, weather, health requirements, conflict, crime, scams, transport reliability, and documentation risk. |
| TRIPSCOPE06 | What must be booked or confirmed? | Identify flights, lodging, transport, appointments, insurance, tickets, tours, storage, movers, and local services. |
| TRIPSCOPE07 | What documents are required? | List passport, visa, permits, IDs, tickets, insurance, invitations, financial proof, school/work letters, and translations. |
| TRIPSCOPE08 | What official sources should be checked? | Use embassy, immigration authority, airline, airport, public health, transport, school, employer, and local government sources. |
| TRIPSCOPE09 | What decisions are pending? | Decide dates, route, budget, visa path, lodging, transport mode, packing strategy, or go/no-go. |
| TRIPSCOPE10 | What travel artifact is required? | Produce trip brief, relocation plan, document checklist, itinerary, or risk note. |
