# Professional Workflow Personal Employment Payroll Benefits Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal employment administration, offer or job records, paystubs, payroll, direct deposit, tax forms, employee benefits, PTO, leave, HR documents, expense reimbursement, work equipment, schedules, time records, performance records, workplace communication, offboarding, workplace issue documentation, or annual employment review.

Use this for personal organization, reminders, recordkeeping, communication drafts, evidence packets, and professional handoff checklists. Do not use it to provide employment-law, tax, immigration, benefits eligibility, medical leave, payroll compliance, union, HR investigation, workplace safety, discrimination, compensation negotiation, or legal advice, and do not replace HR, payroll, benefits administrators, attorneys, tax professionals, clinicians, regulators, unions, or official employer processes.

## EMPSCP - Employment Admin Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| EMPSCP01 | What employment admin situation is being managed? | Define onboarding, payroll, benefits, PTO, reimbursement, schedule, performance records, workplace issue, or offboarding. |
| EMPSCP02 | What employment relationship is involved? | Identify employee, contractor, intern, temporary worker, union role, remote worker, manager, caregiver, or multi-job context. |
| EMPSCP03 | What employer or platform is involved? | Capture employer, staffing agency, payroll provider, benefits portal, timekeeping system, expense system, and HR contact. |

| EMPSCP05 | What documents are authoritative? | Use offer letter, contract, handbook, paystub, benefits guide, HR notice, policy, portal record, or written manager message. |
| EMPSCP06 | What personal boundary applies? | Organize records and questions without giving employment law, tax, immigration, medical leave, or compensation advice. |
| EMPSCP07 | What privacy issue exists? | Protect salary, tax IDs, bank details, health benefits, performance notes, disciplinary records, and coworker information. |
| EMPSCP08 | What connected workflow matters? | Link to tax records, personal finance, health insurance, commute, childcare, credentials, career, or document vault. |
| EMPSCP09 | What success criteria define progress? | Set complete document packet, submitted form, confirmed payment, approved leave, reimbursed expense, or resolved question. |
| EMPSCP10 | What scope artifact should be produced? | Create employment admin brief, HR contact map, deadline tracker, or personal work-record checklist. |
