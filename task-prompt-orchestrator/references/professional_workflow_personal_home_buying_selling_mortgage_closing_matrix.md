# Professional Workflow Personal Home Buying Selling Mortgage Closing Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal home buying, home selling, mortgage preparation, property search, showings, offer packets, inspections, appraisal coordination, title or escrow logistics, closing documents, seller preparation, move-in handoff, property tax or insurance setup, post-closing ownership records, or recurring homeownership review.

Use this for personal organization, checklists, deadline tracking, document gathering, comparison tables, communication drafts, and professional handoff preparation. Do not use it to provide real-estate, legal, mortgage, appraisal, inspection, tax, insurance, investment, title, escrow, construction, zoning, or financial advice, and do not replace a real-estate agent, attorney, lender, mortgage broker, appraiser, home inspector, title company, escrow officer, tax professional, insurer, contractor, surveyor, or government recorder.

## HOMBUY - Home Transaction Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| HOMBUY01 | What home transaction is being organized? | Define buying, selling, refinancing prep, new construction purchase, condo/co-op purchase, investment property admin, or post-closing records. |
| HOMBUY02 | Who participates in the transaction? | Map buyer, seller, co-buyer, agent, lender, attorney, title/escrow, inspector, appraiser, insurer, HOA, and movers. |
| HOMBUY03 | What location and property type matter? | Capture jurisdiction, neighborhood, single-family, condo, co-op, townhouse, multi-unit, land, manufactured home, or new build. |
| HOMBUY04 | What timeline or milestone is driving the work? | Track search start, preapproval, offer, contract, inspection, appraisal, loan approval, closing, possession, move, or sale listing. |
| HOMBUY05 | What budget boundary is known? | Organize user-provided budget, down payment, estimated closing costs, monthly payment comfort, reserves, and advisor questions. |
| HOMBUY06 | What source of truth must be used? | Prefer contract, official disclosures, lender portal, title/escrow instructions, agent emails, inspection report, and county records. |
| HOMBUY07 | What sensitive data is present? | Protect IDs, income, bank statements, tax records, credit reports, loan numbers, wire instructions, addresses, and signatures. |
| HOMBUY08 | What urgency or risk flag exists? | Flag financing contingency, inspection deadline, appraisal issue, title issue, wire instruction, closing date, or possession problem. |
| HOMBUY09 | What should not be decided here? | Do not decide property value, legal rights, loan suitability, tax treatment, inspection severity, or whether to buy/sell. |
| HOMBUY10 | What scope artifact should be produced? | Create transaction brief, milestone map, contact sheet, document inventory, or professional handoff packet. |
