# Professional Workflow Media Entertainment IP Production Distribution Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle media, entertainment, film, video, music, podcasts, publishing, creator operations, IP development, rights, production, post-production, release, distribution, audience growth, monetization, fan communities, media asset systems, or performance learning.

## Media Entertainment Scope

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| MEDIASCOPE01 | What media property or format is involved? | Define film, series, short video, music, podcast, book, comic, game narrative, live stream, newsletter, creator channel, or franchise asset. |
| MEDIASCOPE02 | What business or creative goal applies? | Capture audience growth, revenue, brand lift, cultural impact, franchise development, sponsor value, release milestone, or portfolio learning. |
| MEDIASCOPE03 | Who is the target audience? | Identify demographic, fandom, platform behavior, geography, language, accessibility needs, content expectations, and sensitivity boundaries. |
| MEDIASCOPE04 | What stage is the work in? | Classify development, greenlight, pre-production, production, post, release, distribution, marketing, monetization, archival, or renewal. |
| MEDIASCOPE05 | What platform or channel matters? | Map theatrical, streaming, broadcast, YouTube, TikTok, podcast apps, social, newsletter, bookstore, game store, live venue, or owned site. |
| MEDIASCOPE06 | What creative promise anchors the work? | State genre, tone, format, emotional payoff, audience hook, differentiation, and brand or franchise fit. |
| MEDIASCOPE07 | What constraints shape the project? | Capture budget, schedule, talent availability, union rules, location, rights, platform specs, rating, sponsor rules, and content policy. |
| MEDIASCOPE08 | What safety or sensitivity boundary applies? | Check minors, stunts, harassment, likeness, privacy, cultural representation, misinformation, graphic content, and platform moderation. |
| MEDIASCOPE09 | What source materials exist? | Gather scripts, outlines, bible, footage, audio, art, contracts, rights logs, research, platform analytics, and prior campaign assets. |
| MEDIASCOPE10 | What is out of scope? | Exclude legal, tax, safety, medical, investment, or talent-agent advice unless qualified review and escalation are included. |
