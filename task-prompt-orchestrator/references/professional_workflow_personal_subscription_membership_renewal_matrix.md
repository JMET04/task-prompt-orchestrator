# Professional Workflow Personal Subscription Membership Renewal Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must manage personal subscriptions, memberships, renewals, free trials, auto-pay, app store subscriptions, streaming plans, software plans, cloud storage, gyms, clubs, warranties, recurring donations, account access, family sharing, cancellation, downgrade, pause, receipts, or renewal review. Use this for organization, cost hygiene, reminders, and records, not to replace financial advice, legal advice, consumer regulator decisions, platform policy authority, debt counseling, or business procurement.

## SUBSCP - Subscription Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| SUBSCP01 | What recurring service is in scope? | Define streaming, app, cloud storage, software, gym, club, box, warranty, donation, course, or membership. |
| SUBSCP02 | Why is this subscription being reviewed? | Link to renewal, price increase, unused service, budget reset, account issue, cancellation, family change, or trial end. |
| SUBSCP03 | Who uses the subscription? | Identify self, family, roommate, child, elder, household, team, or shared account user. |
| SUBSCP04 | What job does it perform? | Define entertainment, storage, learning, fitness, productivity, insurance-like coverage, community, convenience, or support. |
| SUBSCP05 | What stakes apply? | Note cost, data lock-in, account access, family dependency, health routine, warranty coverage, or cancellation penalty. |
| SUBSCP06 | What should be excluded? | Exclude legal disputes, regulated financial advice, business procurement, clinical care, and platform policy authority. |
| SUBSCP07 | What time horizon matters? | Consider free trial, monthly cycle, annual renewal, school year, travel period, project term, or seasonal use. |
| SUBSCP08 | What ownership exists? | Clarify payer, account owner, admin, family manager, beneficiary, reimbursement owner, and cancellation authority. |
| SUBSCP09 | What record should be kept? | Track service, plan, price, renewal date, payment method, login owner, cancellation proof, and notes. |
| SUBSCP10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce subscription brief, renewal list, account map, or cancellation plan. |
