# Professional Workflow Personal Shopping Purchase Decision Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must manage personal shopping, product choice, purchase research, comparison, budgeting, channel selection, deals, checkout, delivery, setup, return windows, or post-purchase review. Use this for organization, fit, evidence, and purchase hygiene, not to replace financial advice, legal advice, consumer regulator decisions, product-safety authorities, medical device guidance, or professional procurement.

## PSHSC - Personal Shopping Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| PSHSC01 | What item or category is being considered? | Define product, service, subscription, gift, household item, device, clothing, furniture, course, or experience. |
| PSHSC02 | Why is the purchase needed now? | Link to replacement, upgrade, event, safety, convenience, learning, repair, gift, move, or limited window. |
| PSHSC03 | Who will use it? | Identify user, family member, child, elder, roommate, guest, pet, team, or recipient. |
| PSHSC04 | What job must it do? | Define primary function, frequency, environment, compatibility, durability, portability, style, or comfort need. |
| PSHSC05 | What purchase stakes apply? | Note price, safety, health, privacy, warranty, long-term use, installation, emotional pressure, or return difficulty. |
| PSHSC06 | What should be excluded? | Avoid impulse buys, counterfeit goods, unsafe products, misleading claims, overbudget options, or incompatible accessories. |
| PSHSC07 | What timeline applies? | Set immediate, before event, seasonal sale, replacement deadline, delivery window, or wait-and-watch period. |
| PSHSC08 | What decision authority exists? | Clarify who chooses, pays, approves, receives, returns, or maintains the item. |
| PSHSC09 | What existing item or alternative exists? | Consider repair, borrow, rent, use current item, buy used, share, or skip. |
| PSHSC10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce purchase brief, need statement, comparison plan, or no-buy decision note. |

## BUYCR - Criteria And Tradeoffs

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| BUYCR01 | What criteria rank options? | Score price, quality, reliability, fit, warranty, delivery, reviews, safety, privacy, and support. |
| BUYCR02 | Which criteria are non-negotiable? | Mark dealbreakers for budget, dimensions, safety, compatibility, returnability, availability, or ethical limits. |
| BUYCR03 | Which criteria are flexible? | Allow tradeoffs on brand, color, extra features, speed, packaging, or minor specs. |
| BUYCR04 | What weighting should be used? | Weight essentials more than aesthetics, novelty, discounts, sponsored rankings, or social pressure. |
| BUYCR05 | What risk criteria matter? | Include counterfeits, recalls, weak warranty, privacy exposure, bad seller, difficult returns, or installation risk. |
| BUYCR06 | What maintenance criteria matter? | Check cleaning, repairs, parts, batteries, consumables, updates, storage, and service availability. |
| BUYCR07 | What sustainability or ethics criteria apply? | Consider used/refurbished, repairability, packaging, labor, local purchase, energy use, and disposal. |
| BUYCR08 | What recipient preferences matter? | Include size, color, taste, allergy if user-provided, brand dislikes, accessibility, and cultural sensitivity. |
| BUYCR09 | What criteria conflict must be resolved? | Balance cheap versus durable, fast versus right, premium versus enough, new versus used, or private versus connected. |
| BUYCR10 | What criteria artifact is required? | Produce decision rubric, dealbreaker list, weighted scorecard, or tradeoff note. |

## BUYRS - Research And Source Check

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| BUYRS01 | What sources should be checked? | Use official specs, retailer pages, manuals, independent reviews, forums, recall databases, and user photos. |
| BUYRS02 | Which source is most authoritative? | Prefer manufacturer, standard body, verified seller, official warranty, safety notice, or primary documentation. |
| BUYRS03 | What review signals are trustworthy? | Look for verified purchase, long-term use, consistent complaints, detailed photos, and balanced pros/cons. |
| BUYRS04 | What review signals are suspicious? | Flag review farms, vague praise, copied text, extreme ratings, incentivized posts, or missing negatives. |
| BUYRS05 | What specs must be verified? | Check model number, dimensions, voltage, compatibility, materials, warranty, included parts, and region. |
| BUYRS06 | What safety or recall check is needed? | Search recall notices, safety warnings, age limits, certification marks, and hazard reports. |
| BUYRS07 | What seller reputation should be checked? | Review seller identity, return history, ratings, fulfillment source, counterfeit risk, and support channel. |
| BUYRS08 | What price history should be checked? | Compare normal price, sale price, coupon, used market, seasonal timing, and price tracker when available. |
| BUYRS09 | What research is enough? | Stop when criteria are answered, no major risk remains, and extra research is delaying a reasonable decision. |
| BUYRS10 | What research artifact is required? | Produce source list, spec verification table, risk note, or research summary. |

## BUYCM - Option Comparison

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| BUYCM01 | What options should be compared? | List top candidates, current item, used/refurbished alternative, rental, repair, and no-buy option. |
| BUYCM02 | What comparable fields are needed? | Capture price, specs, dimensions, warranty, return window, shipping, seller, reviews, and compatibility. |
| BUYCM03 | What option is best overall? | Select based on weighted criteria, not just cheapest, most famous, or most advertised. |
| BUYCM04 | What option is best value? | Compare total benefit against total cost, expected use, durability, and maintenance. |
| BUYCM05 | What option is safest or lowest risk? | Prefer reliable seller, strong return policy, verified specs, known support, and fewer hidden dependencies. |
| BUYCM06 | What option should be rejected? | Reject incompatible, unsafe, overbudget, poor reviews, weak warranty, or suspicious listing. |
| BUYCM07 | What uncertainty remains? | Note unknown fit, real color, long-term durability, seller reliability, delivery timing, or installation effort. |
| BUYCM08 | What test or trial can reduce uncertainty? | Try in store, borrow, rent, order with return, check measurements, or ask seller questions. |
| BUYCM09 | What decision threshold applies? | Buy, wait, research more, repair, borrow, rent, or skip based on criteria and urgency. |
| BUYCM10 | What comparison artifact is required? | Produce comparison table, finalist shortlist, recommendation, or no-buy decision. |
