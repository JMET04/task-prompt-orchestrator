# Professional Workflow End Of Life Funeral Memorial Bereavement Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle end-of-life preferences, serious-illness transition coordination, death notification logistics, funeral or memorial planning, obituary or tribute writing, grief support organization, after-death household administration, document gathering, digital legacy, condolence communication, cultural or faith-informed rituals, or bereavement review. This matrix organizes logistics and communication; it does not replace medical care, hospice expertise, legal advice, estate administration, licensed funeral services, clergy/faith leadership, financial advice, or mental-health crisis support.

## End Of Life And Bereavement Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| EOLSCOPE01 | What end-of-life or bereavement situation is in scope? | Define serious illness planning, hospice transition, recent death, funeral, memorial, grief support, or after-death admin. |
| EOLSCOPE02 | Who is the central person or family system? | Identify patient, deceased person, spouse/partner, parent, child, executor, caregiver, next of kin, or chosen family. |
| EOLSCOPE03 | What timing and urgency apply? | Separate immediate death response, next 24 hours, service planning, administrative month, and longer grief support. |
| EOLSCOPE04 | What professional boundary applies? | Route medical, hospice, legal, estate, tax, insurance, funeral licensing, and crisis concerns to qualified professionals. |
| EOLSCOPE05 | What cultural, faith, or personal practice matters? | Capture rituals, language, food, dress, music, body care, timing, burial/cremation preference, and community expectations. |
| EOLSCOPE06 | What privacy and dignity boundary matters? | Protect health information, family conflict, images, finances, cause of death, minors, and sensitive history. |
| EOLSCOPE07 | What authority or role must be clarified? | Identify decision-maker, executor, next of kin, healthcare proxy, funeral contact, communicator, and document holder. |
| EOLSCOPE08 | What resources and constraints exist? | Note budget, location, travel, venue, family availability, service providers, documents, and emotional capacity. |
| EOLSCOPE09 | What support need is visible? | Identify practical help, emotional support, childcare, meals, transport, translation, accessibility, or crisis support. |
| EOLSCOPE10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce situation brief, role map, professional-boundary note, priority list, or bereavement coordination charter. |

## Death Notification And Immediate Next Steps

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| DEATHNOT01 | What immediate notification is needed? | Identify medical certifier, hospice/facility contact, funeral provider, next of kin, executor, close family, or faith leader. |
| DEATHNOT02 | What official process must be followed? | Follow local rules for pronouncement, death certificate, body transport, reporting, and funeral provider instructions. |
| DEATHNOT03 | What emergency boundary applies? | Use emergency services for unexpected death, suspicious circumstances, unattended death, danger, or legal requirement. |
| DEATHNOT04 | Who should be told first? | Prioritize spouse/partner, children, parents, siblings, chosen family, executor, caregiving team, and key community contacts. |
| DEATHNOT05 | What message tone is appropriate? | Draft brief, factual, compassionate wording with limited details and clear next-step expectations. |
| DEATHNOT06 | What should not be shared yet? | Avoid cause details, rumors, family conflict, financial data, service details before confirmed, and graphic information. |
| DEATHNOT07 | What contact chain helps? | Assign callers, text/email owners, translation support, social-media hold, and people who need personal calls. |
| DEATHNOT08 | What practical first tasks exist? | Secure home, care for dependents/pets, gather documents, contact provider, pause appointments, and arrange support. |
| DEATHNOT09 | What record should be kept? | Track who was notified, when, by whom, response, travel needs, and follow-up promises. |
| DEATHNOT10 | What notification artifact is required? | Produce call tree, notification script, first-24-hours checklist, contact log, or privacy note. |
