# Professional Workflow Supply Chain Logistics Inventory Fulfillment Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must plan, source, allocate, store, move, fulfill, track, return, recover, or optimize physical goods, inventory, logistics capacity, supply constraints, order fulfillment, warehouse operations, transportation, import/export flow, service parts, retail/ecommerce stock, or supply-chain resilience.

## Supply Chain Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| SCSCOPE01 | What supply-chain outcome is in scope? | Define availability, service level, cost, lead time, resilience, quality, compliance, sustainability, or customer promise. |
| SCSCOPE02 | What product or material flow is involved? | Identify raw material, component, finished good, spare part, sample, prototype, packaging, consumable, or return. |
| SCSCOPE03 | What channel or fulfillment model applies? | Choose wholesale, retail, ecommerce, marketplace, direct-to-consumer, B2B, distributor, drop ship, subscription, or field service. |
| SCSCOPE04 | What geography and node network matters? | Map supplier, factory, port, warehouse, store, carrier hub, customer region, repair center, or disposal site. |
| SCSCOPE05 | What planning horizon applies? | Separate daily execution, weekly replenishment, monthly S&OP, seasonal build, launch ramp, or long-range capacity plan. |
| SCSCOPE06 | What customer promise must be protected? | Define delivery date, fill rate, availability, freshness, installation window, repair SLA, or launch date. |
| SCSCOPE07 | What constraints shape the flow? | Capture lead time, MOQ, shelf life, capacity, labor, transport mode, customs, regulation, budget, and storage limits. |
| SCSCOPE08 | What system of record applies? | Identify ERP, WMS, TMS, OMS, POS, marketplace, spreadsheet, supplier portal, or carrier platform. |
| SCSCOPE09 | What supply-chain artifact is required? | Produce flow map, node map, planning brief, service promise, constraint register, or operating model. |
| SCSCOPE10 | What proves the supply-chain workflow is working? | Use availability, on-time delivery, inventory accuracy, cost, exception closure, customer impact, and audit evidence. |
