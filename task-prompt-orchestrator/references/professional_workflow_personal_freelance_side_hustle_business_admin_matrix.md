# Professional Workflow Personal Freelance Side Hustle Business Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal freelance work, side-hustle administration, solo service business, gig work records, client intake, quotes, proposals, statements of work, contract handoff, project tracking, invoices, payment collection, expenses, tax records, platform profiles, client communications, boundaries, or recurring freelance business review.

Use this for personal organization, client records, templates, checklists, communication drafts, payment tracking, evidence packets, tax-prep records, and professional handoff preparation. Do not use it to provide legal, tax, accounting, investment, employment-law, insurance, licensing, regulated-profession, debt-collection, or fiduciary advice; do not replace attorneys, accountants, tax preparers, business advisors, platforms, payment processors, insurers, or regulators.

| FRLSCP06 | What work lifecycle stage is active? | Track lead, inquiry, quote, proposal, SOW, kickoff, delivery, revision, invoice, payment, maintenance, or closeout. |
