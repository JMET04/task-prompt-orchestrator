# Professional Workflow Personal Finance Tax Wealth Admin Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal finance organization, household cash flow, budgeting systems, bills, debt coordination, savings goals, tax-document preparation, insurance administration, estate-document organization, credit records, financial security, family money coordination, major purchase planning, or periodic finance reviews. This matrix supports organization, education, and professional handoff; it does not provide investment, tax, insurance, legal, or fiduciary advice.

| PFINSCOPE06 | What time horizon matters? | Choose weekly cash flow, monthly budget, annual tax prep, life event, loan term, policy renewal, retirement checkpoint, or estate update. |

| PFINSCOPE10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce finance scope note, data request list, advice-boundary note, professional handoff checklist, or task plan. |
