# Professional Workflow Secondhand Resale Circular Goods Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal secondhand selling, used-goods buying, decluttering, resale, giveaway, donation, swap, repair-for-resale, local pickup, small marketplace listings, buyer/seller screening, safe payment, shipping, scams, disputes, or circular reuse decisions. This matrix supports personal and occasional transactions; it does not replace legal, tax, platform enforcement, product-safety, authentication, customs, or regulated-commerce advice.

## Secondhand Resale Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| REUSESCP01 | What item or lot is in scope? | Define used item, bundle, collectible, appliance, furniture, clothing, electronics, vehicle accessory, book, tool, or giveaway. |
| REUSESCP02 | What transaction type applies? | Choose sell, buy, trade, donate, give away, consign, recycle, repair first, or keep. |
| REUSESCP03 | What personal goal matters? | Optimize space, speed, money recovery, waste reduction, safe handoff, good home, or minimal effort. |
| REUSESCP04 | What platform or channel is considered? | Identify local marketplace, social group, marketplace app, consignment, charity, curb alert, or private network. |
| REUSESCP05 | What regulated or restricted boundary applies? | Flag weapons, medication, food, cosmetics, baby gear, safety equipment, recalled goods, tickets, animals, or counterfeit risk. |
| REUSESCP06 | What privacy issue exists? | Remove addresses, accounts, photos, personal labels, documents, device data, and location metadata. |
| REUSESCP07 | What transaction risk level exists? | Assess item value, buyer/seller unknown, meeting location, payment method, shipping risk, and scam pattern. |
| REUSESCP08 | What time constraint exists? | Account for move-out, cleanup deadline, seasonal demand, storage cost, pickup window, or buyer urgency. |
| REUSESCP09 | What proof or record is needed? | Keep listing, photos, messages, payment receipt, tracking, condition notes, and pickup confirmation. |
| REUSESCP10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce item plan, channel decision, safety boundary, transaction brief, or declutter priority list. |

## Item Condition Cleaning And Prep

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| ITEMCOND01 | What is the item's true condition? | Record new, like new, good, fair, parts-only, damaged, missing pieces, odor, wear, stains, or unknown function. |
| ITEMCOND02 | What function test is needed? | Check power, buttons, fit, moving parts, accessories, battery, charging, dimensions, and known defects. |
| ITEMCOND03 | What cleaning or reset is appropriate? | Clean surfaces, launder, dust, disinfect where safe, factory reset, remove stickers, and empty compartments. |
| ITEMCOND04 | What repair is worth doing? | Decide simple fix, missing accessory, battery, polishing, patching, or sell as-is based on cost and risk. |
| ITEMCOND05 | What safety issue should stop sale? | Do not sell recalled, unsafe, contaminated, counterfeit, broken safety gear, or hazardous items. |
| ITEMCOND06 | What accessories should be included? | List manuals, chargers, cables, boxes, receipts, spare parts, mounts, cases, and original packaging. |
| ITEMCOND07 | What measurements matter? | Capture size, weight, model number, serial if safe to share, capacity, dimensions, color, and compatibility. |
| ITEMCOND08 | What photos should show condition honestly? | Photograph front, back, labels, defects, scale, included parts, and proof of function without personal data. |
| ITEMCOND09 | What condition wording is fair? | Use clear, non-hyped description, disclose flaws, avoid unsupported claims, and state what was tested. |
| ITEMCOND10 | What prep artifact is required? | Produce condition checklist, photo list, cleaning log, included-parts list, or as-is disclosure. |

| BUYSCREEN09 | What child or dependent safety concern exists? | Avoid involving minors in pickup, protect family schedules, and keep home details private. |

| MEETSAFE09 | What post-pickup confirmation is needed? | Mark sold, archive messages, confirm receipt, remove listing, and update inventory. |
