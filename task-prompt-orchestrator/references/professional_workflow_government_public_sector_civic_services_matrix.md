# Professional Workflow Government Public Sector Civic Services Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle government, public-sector, civic-service, public-benefit, licensing, permitting, grants, procurement, citizen services, public communications, emergency management, transparency, or digital-government operations.

## Public Sector Scope And Mandate

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| GOVSCOPE01 | What public-sector function is involved? | Define agency service, policy program, permitting, licensing, grants, procurement, public engagement, enforcement, emergency response, or digital service. |
| GOVSCOPE02 | What legal or policy mandate applies? | Identify statute, regulation, ordinance, executive policy, program rule, grant condition, service charter, or public authority. |
| GOVSCOPE03 | Who are the public stakeholders? | Map residents, applicants, regulated entities, elected officials, agency staff, community groups, vendors, oversight bodies, media, and vulnerable users. |
| GOVSCOPE04 | What public value outcome is expected? | Define access, fairness, safety, compliance, service speed, economic development, transparency, resilience, public trust, or cost stewardship. |
| GOVSCOPE05 | What jurisdiction and boundary apply? | Record geography, agency authority, interagency dependencies, eligibility limits, privacy boundary, records boundary, and appeal rights. |
| GOVSCOPE06 | What equity and accessibility obligations apply? | Account for disability access, language access, digital access, low-literacy users, rural access, economic barriers, and civil-rights impacts. |
| GOVSCOPE07 | What public risk level applies? | Rate health/safety, legal exposure, benefit denial, enforcement impact, procurement integrity, political sensitivity, and reputational risk. |
| GOVSCOPE08 | What service standard must be met? | Define statutory deadlines, SLA, response time, publication cadence, escalation path, and exception rules. |
| GOVSCOPE09 | What evidence must support action? | Identify application data, official records, inspections, eligibility documents, public comments, financial records, and decision rationale. |
| GOVSCOPE10 | What is out of scope? | Exclude unlicensed legal advice, political campaigning, undisclosed lobbying, unauthorized access, biased eligibility decisions, or unsupported official claims. |
