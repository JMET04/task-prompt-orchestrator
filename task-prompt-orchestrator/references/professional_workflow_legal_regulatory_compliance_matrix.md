# Professional Workflow Legal Regulatory Compliance Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow may be shaped by laws, regulations, contracts, approvals, disclosures, claims, regulated industries, public-facing promises, compliance operations, investigations, or enforcement exposure. This matrix structures review and escalation; it does not replace qualified legal advice.

## Legal Regulatory Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| LEGSCOP01 | What activity may create legal or regulatory exposure? | Identify publishing, selling, advising, collecting data, deploying automation, contracting, hiring, manufacturing, importing, or regulated service activity. |
| LEGSCOP02 | Who is affected by the legal obligation? | Map customers, users, employees, contractors, vendors, partners, investors, regulators, minors, patients, citizens, or public audiences. |
| LEGSCOP03 | What jurisdictional footprint exists? | Capture countries, states, regions, delivery locations, data locations, users, entities, and governing law. |
| LEGSCOP04 | What regulated domain applies? | Flag finance, health, education, employment, housing, insurance, legal services, public sector, consumer protection, environment, safety, or telecom. |
| LEGSCOP05 | What legal status does the artifact have? | Distinguish draft, internal guidance, public claim, contract, policy, regulated disclosure, official record, or legal notice. |
| LEGSCOP06 | What legal decision must be avoided by non-lawyers? | Route interpretations, rights determinations, litigation strategy, formal notices, and regulated advice to qualified counsel. |
| LEGSCOP07 | What user disclaimer or boundary is needed? | Define when outputs must state limitations, non-advice status, source limits, or counsel-review requirement. |
| LEGSCOP08 | What existing policies already govern the work? | Link company policy, compliance manuals, approval matrices, standard terms, and regulator guidance. |
| LEGSCOP09 | What legal risk rating applies? | Triage low, moderate, high, or critical risk using impact, audience, reversibility, regulator exposure, and precedent. |
| LEGSCOP10 | What legal review artifact is required? | Decide whether to produce issue list, compliance checklist, counsel brief, approval log, or signoff packet. |
