# Professional Workflow Personal Dining Restaurant Takeout Food Delivery Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle personal dining, restaurant choice, reservations, takeout, food delivery, menus, dietary restrictions, group meals, payments, tipping, dining etiquette, complaints, leftovers, or recurring dining review.

Use this for personal planning, coordination, communication, budgeting, and records. Do not use it to replace medical nutrition care, allergy treatment, food-safety authority decisions, legal advice, restaurant licensing enforcement, emergency services, or professional event catering.

## DINSCP - Dining Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| DINSCP01 | What dining situation is in scope? | Identify dine-in, takeout, delivery, drive-through, cafe, group meal, date, family meal, travel meal, or celebration. |
| DINSCP02 | Who is eating? | Capture number of people, ages, dietary needs, accessibility, budget roles, relationship context, and guest expectations. |
| DINSCP03 | What outcome is desired? | Produce restaurant shortlist, reservation plan, order sheet, group coordination, complaint packet, or dining review. |
| DINSCP04 | What time constraint applies? | Note meal time, reservation window, delivery deadline, showtime, work break, child bedtime, or travel schedule. |
| DINSCP05 | What location constraint applies? | Consider neighborhood, distance, parking, transit, delivery zone, hotel area, venue proximity, or safe pickup. |
| DINSCP06 | What budget expectation applies? | Set per-person target, total cap, split rule, tax/tip/delivery fees, coupons, or reimbursement. |
| DINSCP07 | What risk or sensitivity exists? | Flag allergies, religious/cultural restrictions, accessibility, illness, privacy, sobriety, safety, or high-stakes occasion. |
| DINSCP08 | What source should guide choices? | Use menu, restaurant site, reviews, official hours, reservation app, delivery app, and direct confirmation. |
| DINSCP09 | What boundary keeps the workflow safe? | Do not provide medical nutrition treatment, allergy guarantees, legal claims, or food-safety enforcement conclusions. |
| DINSCP10 | What artifact should be produced? | Create dining brief, option shortlist, order plan, reservation packet, or post-meal note. |

## DIETRY - Dietary Allergy Preference

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| DIETRY01 | What dietary restriction applies? | Capture allergy, intolerance, medical diet, religious rule, vegetarian/vegan, pregnancy caution, or preference. |
| DIETRY02 | How severe is the restriction? | Distinguish preference, avoidance, intolerance, allergy, cross-contact concern, and medical emergency risk. |
| DIETRY03 | What must be confirmed with the restaurant? | Ask ingredients, preparation, fryer, sauce, substitutions, cross-contact, labeling, and staff confidence. |
| DIETRY04 | What safe fallback exists? | Prepare alternate restaurant, simple dish, packed snack, grocery option, or skip-order plan. |
| DIETRY05 | What language should be used? | Create clear allergy card, dietary note, translation, or concise server message. |
| DIETRY06 | What should not be assumed? | Do not infer allergen safety, religious compliance, gluten-free safety, or medical suitability from menu names alone. |
| DIETRY07 | What group coordination is needed? | Share restrictions early, choose inclusive venue, avoid pressure, and plan separate orders if needed. |
| DIETRY08 | What emergency boundary applies? | Route exposure, reaction, choking, or medical symptoms to emergency/medical guidance. |
| DIETRY09 | What record should be kept? | Save restaurant notes, safe dishes, bad experiences, allergy communication, and preferred orders. |
| DIETRY10 | What dietary artifact should be produced? | Create dietary brief, restaurant question script, allergy card, or safe-order list. |

## COMPDN - Dining Complaint Correction

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| COMPDN01 | What problem occurred? | Classify wrong item, missing item, allergy concern, poor service, delay, cold food, billing error, or safety concern. |
| COMPDN02 | What evidence is available? | Gather receipt, order number, photos, timestamps, messages, delivery proof, and item details. |
| COMPDN03 | What resolution is requested? | Ask for remake, refund, credit, missing item, apology, explanation, or future prevention. |
| COMPDN04 | Who should be contacted? | Choose server, manager, restaurant, delivery app, payment provider, or corporate support. |
| COMPDN05 | What language stays constructive? | Use facts, impact, desired resolution, and calm tone without threats or public escalation first. |
| COMPDN06 | What food-safety escalation may be needed? | Route illness, foreign object, contamination, or severe allergy exposure to medical or official channels as appropriate. |
| COMPDN07 | What deadline applies? | Note app refund window, same-day correction, charge dispute window, or manager follow-up. |
| COMPDN08 | What should be avoided? | Avoid unsupported accusations, legal conclusions, harassment, or unsafe consumption. |
| COMPDN09 | What follow-up should be tracked? | Save case number, promised credit, refund status, replacement, and final outcome. |
| COMPDN10 | What complaint artifact should be produced? | Create complaint message, evidence packet, refund tracker, or dining issue log. |

## LEFTDN - Leftovers Food Handling

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| LEFTDN01 | What leftovers exist? | Identify dish, quantity, time packed, temperature concern, container, and who will use it. |
| LEFTDN02 | What storage is needed? | Refrigerate, freeze, separate sauce, label date, avoid leakage, and protect from cross-contact. |
| LEFTDN03 | What food safety boundary applies? | Avoid unsafe time/temperature assumptions; discard questionable food and route illness to medical guidance. |
| LEFTDN04 | What reheating instruction helps? | Note oven, microwave, stovetop, air fryer, sauce addition, or do-not-reheat item. |
| LEFTDN05 | What item should not be saved? | Avoid risky seafood, buffet leftovers, unrefrigerated food, soggy fragile items, or allergy-uncertain dishes. |
| LEFTDN06 | What waste reduction option exists? | Plan next meal, share safely, compost, order less next time, or choose durable dishes. |
| LEFTDN07 | What container or label is needed? | Use sealed container, name label, date, allergen note, and reheating note. |
| LEFTDN08 | What delivery packaging issue exists? | Transfer from flimsy, leaky, or non-reheatable packaging to safe storage. |
| LEFTDN09 | What family communication is needed? | Mark who owns leftovers, when to eat, allergen note, and discard date. |
| LEFTDN10 | What leftover artifact should be produced? | Create leftover label, next-meal plan, waste note, or ordering adjustment. |
