# Professional Workflow Event Hospitality Travel Experience Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must plan or operate events, conferences, trade shows, workshops, performances, hospitality programs, travel itineraries, lodging, ticketing, guest services, venue operations, destination experiences, or live on-site experiences.

## Event Scope And Experience Strategy

| Code | Question | Prompt/workflow requirement |
|---|---|---|
| EVSCOPE01 | What type of experience is being created? | Define conference, meeting, exhibition, launch, workshop, ceremony, performance, tour, retreat, hospitality program, or hybrid event. |
| EVSCOPE02 | Who is the primary guest or attendee? | Identify audience segments, VIPs, speakers, sponsors, exhibitors, staff, vendors, media, families, accessibility needs, and risk groups. |
| EVSCOPE03 | What outcome must the experience deliver? | Record business, learning, networking, brand, revenue, fundraising, cultural, community, employee, or customer-success goals. |
| EVSCOPE04 | What guest journey must be shaped? | Map invite, registration, travel, arrival, check-in, participation, breaks, departures, follow-up, and memory moments. |
| EVSCOPE05 | What format and scale apply? | Define in-person, virtual, hybrid, single-site, multi-site, pop-up, recurring, ticketed, private, public, small, medium, or mass scale. |
| EVSCOPE06 | What constraints set the design envelope? | Capture budget, date, season, venue capacity, travel distance, staffing, permits, technical limits, sponsor obligations, and brand rules. |
| EVSCOPE07 | What success signals matter? | Define attendance, satisfaction, revenue, engagement, leads, repeat bookings, safety, schedule adherence, service quality, and post-event impact. |
| EVSCOPE08 | What cultural or local context matters? | Account for local customs, holidays, language, etiquette, accessibility norms, safety norms, climate, transport patterns, and community impact. |
| EVSCOPE09 | What is the experience promise? | State the guest-facing value proposition, mood, service standard, pacing, hospitality tone, and differentiating moment. |
| EVSCOPE10 | What is out of scope? | Exclude unsupported locations, unsafe activities, unapproved claims, uncontracted services, legal advice, medical advice, or unverified guarantees. |
