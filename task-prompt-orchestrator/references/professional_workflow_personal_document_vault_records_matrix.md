# Professional Workflow Personal Document Vault Records Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must manage personal documents, document vaults, IDs, records, scans, backups, expiration dates, proof packets, household records, forms, secure sharing, or archival cleanup.

This supports organization, privacy, retrieval, and handoff planning. It does not replace legal advice, tax advice, insurance advice, medical records advice, government agency decisions, notarization authority, or cybersecurity incident response.

## Document Vault Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| DOCVSC01 | What document workflow is needed? | Classify vault setup, record cleanup, scan project, form prep, proof packet, backup, sharing, expiration tracking, or archive review. |
| DOCVSC02 | Whose documents are included? | Identify self, spouse/partner, child, elder, dependent, household, business owner, or estate-related owner. |
| DOCVSC03 | What document categories are in scope? | Include identity, finance, tax, insurance, health, school, property, vehicle, employment, legal, warranty, and digital records. |
| DOCVSC04 | What risk drives the work? | Note lost documents, deadline, emergency, move, travel, application, disaster readiness, fraud, or family handoff. |
| DOCVSC05 | What privacy level applies? | Mark public, routine private, sensitive, regulated, identity-risk, financial-risk, medical, or legal-sensitive. |
| DOCVSC06 | What source should be authoritative? | Prefer official issuer, original document, signed agreement, current portal, regulator, insurer, employer, or school. |
| DOCVSC07 | What access boundary matters? | Separate owner, viewer, editor, emergency contact, caregiver, attorney, accountant, and executor roles. |
| DOCVSC08 | What should not be stored casually? | Avoid unencrypted IDs, passwords, full card numbers, unnecessary medical details, and private third-party data. |
| DOCVSC09 | What lifecycle stage applies? | Classify active, reference, expiring, replaced, archived, shared, disposed, or emergency-ready. |
| DOCVSC10 | What scope artifact is required? | Produce vault map, document inventory, risk note, owner list, or cleanup plan. |
