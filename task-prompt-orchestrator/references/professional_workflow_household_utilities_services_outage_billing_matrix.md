# Professional Workflow Household Utilities Services Outage Billing Matrix

Use this matrix when a workflow must handle household utilities and home service accounts such as electricity, gas, water, sewer, trash, recycling, internet, phone, service setup, transfers, meter readings, bills, outages, repairs, appointments, conservation, move-in or move-out utility coordination, or recurring service review.

Use this for personal household administration and coordination. Do not use it to replace utility company authority, licensed electrical/plumbing/gas work, emergency services, legal advice, public-utility commission rulings, or professional energy engineering.

## HUTSCP - Household Utilities Scope

| Code | Workflow question | Workflow use |
|---|---|---|
| HUTSCP01 | What service is in scope? | Identify electricity, gas, water, sewer, trash, recycling, internet, phone, security, or bundled home service. |
| HUTSCP02 | What property context applies? | Capture owned home, rental, apartment, dorm, shared housing, short-term stay, second home, or move transition. |
| HUTSCP03 | What outcome is needed? | Produce setup plan, bill audit, outage response, service appointment plan, transfer checklist, or account review. |
| HUTSCP04 | Who is responsible for the service? | Identify resident, landlord, roommate, HOA, property manager, utility provider, installer, or city service. |
| HUTSCP05 | What urgency applies? | Mark emergency, safety issue, active outage, shutoff notice, billing deadline, move date, or routine review. |
| HUTSCP06 | What account evidence exists? | Gather bills, account numbers, meter photos, emails, lease clauses, service agreements, and confirmation numbers. |
| HUTSCP07 | What safety boundary applies? | Route gas smell, sparks, flooding, exposed wires, sewer backup, and carbon monoxide alarms to emergency guidance. |
| HUTSCP08 | What official source should be followed? | Use provider portal, city service page, lease, landlord notice, utility alerts, or regulator information. |
| HUTSCP09 | What privacy boundary applies? | Protect address, account number, access codes, meter photos, billing details, and occupancy routines. |
| HUTSCP10 | What artifact should be produced? | Create utility dashboard, service checklist, outage log, bill tracker, or move utility packet. |
